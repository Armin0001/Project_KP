Kamberovića polje

Kamberovića polje is one of Zenica's centraol urban areas located next to river Bosna. This park hosts cirkuses and amusment parks. Thousands of Zenica citizens gather every 21st of March in this park to mark the first day of Spring. This event is known as Čimburijada. With many sports and recreational places, Kamberovica polje is very dear amoung citizens of Zenica & the guests of Zenica. Beside a walking trail, kids grounds, there are also tennis courts, chess area, aero-model, volleyball, handball, basketball, football, rugby and atlethic facilities.

The athletic stadium has an area of 3858 meter squared and has the capacity of 3000.
Kamberovica polje stadium is mostly used for atletic competitions, Bosnia and Herzegovina's national rugby team play their games in this stadium and Zenica's rugby teams as well. Also, football games of local teams which are in lower ranks of competition are played here.

Shopping center covers more than 60 000 square meters on three ground levels. The shopping center hosts many domestic and foreign companies.

Arena Zenica is a multifunctional arena in Zenica, primarily used for hosting sports events.

Hotel Zenica is located in the heart of Zenica. Near Hotel Zenica there are many attractions. With lot of space in rooms, which are designed to meet the guest requirements, they offer a good place to stay for those who plan to visit Zenica for business purposes but also as a tourist.

There are many playgrounds for people to enjoy in the area.

Kid playgrounds are also part of Kamberovica polje.

Kamberovica polje in general offers a great place for running. One section which can be used for running is next to coast of river Bosna.
